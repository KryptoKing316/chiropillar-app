The hook is everything.
You have 3 seconds.
Backed by the @mitcheil framework — every hook is pattern-matched from content that has already done 10M+ views. Break the doom scroll or lose them forever.
Hook A — Recommended · The Contrast Hook
"Legacy brokers take 18 months and charge 12%. Our AI closes in 60 days for 3%."
Open on a split screen — left: broker on phone shuffling papers. Right: KB dashboard matching deals live. Audio and visual match one-to-one. Stops the scroll because the contrast is visceral.

One misalignment loses trust. One word the viewer doesn't understand and they're gone. Write at a fifth-grade reading level.
